About REDAN Enugu

Why Choose REDAN Enugu

REDAN Enugu Chapter is the regional arm of the Real Estate Developers Association of Nigeria, officially recognized by the Federal Government as the principal umbrella body for organized real estate development in the Southeast. We champion sustainable housing development, professional standards, and strategic partnerships across Enugu State and the Southeast region.

We provide comprehensive support through professional certification programs, advocacy for favorable policies, access to financing opportunities via partnerships with institutions like FMBN, and continuous training on industry best practices. Our members benefit from networking opportunities, technical assistance, and market intelligence tailored to the Southeast region.

REDAN Enugu Chapter maintains rigorous quality standards through our certification process, regular project inspections, and adherence to sustainable building practices. We integrate innovative technologies, green initiatives, and smart solutions to ensure all developments meet international standards while addressing local housing needs.

Our services include member certification and training, policy advocacy, project financing facilitation, technical consultancy, market research and intelligence, networking events, and partnerships with government agencies and financial institutions. We focus on affordable homeownership, mortgage accessibility, and sustainable urban development across the Southeast.
